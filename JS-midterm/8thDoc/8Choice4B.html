Doctor Who: 8th Doctor Adventures

Time of the Revolution

The two travelers embark on a journey in a carriage. The journey is filled with joy from the people they are traveling with, but it is long. Very long in fact. As the days grow shorter Charley just wishes that the TARDIS landed in Singapore like it was supposed to. Even the Doctor got so sick of the ride he started sleeping at night. While this trip is barely longer than the boat trip would have taken, this trip is not relaxing. At least the food is freshly hunted and cooked every night.
