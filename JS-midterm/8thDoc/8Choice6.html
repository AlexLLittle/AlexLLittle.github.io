Doctor Who: 8th Doctor Adventures

Time of the Revolution

“Now surely you already know this Mr. Paine but your writing is truly splendid! It's persuasive, clear and direct. Without your work with Common Sense I don’t even think this revolution would have started!” The Doctor was in full geek mode, while his tone of voice was the same as normal, the speed of which he spoke was much faster. Charley just stood there, not particularly shocked at the Doctor's excitement, but evidently not wanting to be there.

“You really think so?”

“I do. Now I don’t necessarily agree with every point you made, but it is true that Britain under the reign of King George III is tyrannical.”

“Are you a fighter, Doctor?”

“Me? No. I run away from war. I much prefer to travel, and appreciate what is, but sometimes,when you are faced with oppression and tyranny, there is no other choice.”

“And you agree that Britain is oppressing us?”

“I do suppose I do”

“So join our fight!”

“I’m afraid I can’t do that. I’m merely a traveler here to appreciate your work”

“If you don’t mind me asking where are you from Doctor?”

“Very far away, Mr. Paine. If you don’t mind I’d like to discuss more of your work before you need to leave” The Doctor and Thomas Paine continued to talk for what seemed like years for Charley, first starting with Paine’s work African Slavery in America condemning the enslavement of other humans, before returning to talking about Common Sense. Eventually it was time for the newly found friends to separate, “well Mr. Paine, this was an insightful discussion about the literary art of persuasion and of human ethics, but I imagine you must get going soon”

“Very true Doctor, it was a pleasure to talk with someone else so insightful, don’t be afraid to drop by again, and do call me Thomas”

“Of course my friend. I am sure that this revolution going on will end very well for the 13 colonies.”

“I do hope so Doctor, I really do” As Thomas Paine left Charley walked back to the Doctor,

“Can we leave now Doctor?”

“I’ve never seen you so continuously eager to leave Charley”

“Nothing has happened! I hate being kidnapped and all, but it at least gives me something to do”

“I’m sure I can find something for you to do here in Philadelphia so that your time here wasn’t completely wasted if you would like”
