Doctor Who: 8th Doctor Adventures

Time of the Revolution

The Doctor and Charley wait out front until they see Davros, the creator of the Daleks fly out of the TARDIS

“What the-” The 8th Doctor says running towards Davros

“Not another one of you!” Davros says before a future incarnation of the Doctor rushes out of the TARDIS to grab Davro’s chair. Following behind him is Sarah Jane Smith.

“I was hoping that the TARDIS locked on to a more proactive self than you” the future doctor said, “anyways you know-”

“Why if it isn’t Sarah Jane Smith. I can tell it's been a while since the last time I saw you, what are you doing traveling with chiny and what is going on?”

“Actually I can explain-”

“Shut up chiny. I trust Sarah to explain much better than you”

“Well, we accidentally landed on Skaro, and after a bit the Doctor decided to try and trick Davros onto the TARDIS taking him as far away from the time war as possible to save millions of lives”

“The time war?”

“You haven’t gotten there yet” the other Doctor interjected, “you will soon if we don’t end Davros here and now” Davros begins to squirm around in the chair until he gets his hands on the control and spins it, taking both Doctors by surprise and starts flying off into town”

“Great” the 8th Doctor started, “now we have let Davros loose in revolutionary America”

“Well what are we going to do Doctor? I’m rather confused on what's going on” Charley finally said after standing there watching.

“Well Charley, this isn’t our fight. It is chin’s fight”

“I brought my TARDIS here just so that two of us can be fighting Davros, to truly defeat him”
