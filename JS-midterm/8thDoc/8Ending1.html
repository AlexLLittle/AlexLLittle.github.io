Time of the Revolution

“Well Charley” the Doctor started to say while opening the door to the TARDIS, “I’m sorry that there wasn’t much for you to do here”

“It's okay Doctor, let's just make sure we get to Singapore 1930 next!”

“That all depends on our luck Charley”

“I thought you weren’t a big believer in luck Doctor”

“I never said that Charley, or never meant it like that. Luck is just a very human concept”

“Is it really?”

“No. Not really, Singapore, here we come!”

The End
