Doctor Who: 8th Doctor Adventures

Time of the Revolution

“Well Charley, we have finally made it!”

“About time, the sooner you meet Thomas Paine, the sooner we get to eat proper meals again…”

“If my history is correct, and it usually is, we have just barely missed a continental congress, but that means Thomas Paine should be leaving soon”

“Do you even know what he looks like?”

“Of course I do! He is one of the most influential writers of this time. Maybe of all time or even all of Earth! I know what Thomas Paine looks like!” A man approaches from behind and stands there, Charley nods towards him and the Doctor turns to face him, “Oh hello sir, who might you be?”

“I am Thomas Paine. Now I might inquirer as to who you are” once more Charley starts to chuckle before saying,

“I thought you knew what he looked like?”

“I’ve only seen paintings, Charley. He looks very different in person”

“He is standing right there”

“The lady is right. Who are you?”

“I am the Doctor, and this is my traveling companion Charley Pollard”

“What do you want with me Mr. Doctor?”

“Just Doctor is fine and well I am a big fan really”

“Doctor…” Charley interrupts to prevent the Doctor from making a complete fool of himself.

“What I mean Mr. Paine, uh Thomas if I may-”

“You may not. Lets stick with formalities please Doctor”

“Uh, right, Mr. Paine. What I meant to say before I got carried away is that I know of your wonderful writing and its great influence on what will become the American people and of this revolution you all have going on right now, and I have so much that I would love to ask you!” Charley leans over to the Doctor and quietly says,

“Very cool Doctor…” in a sarcastic tone.

“What was that Miss Pollard?”

“Nothing Mr. Paine”

“So what do you think Mr. Paine, do you have time in your busy day to exchange a few words with a humble traveler like myself?”

“Sure but let's make it quick I have work that needs to be done”

What should the Doctor discuss with Thomas Paine?
