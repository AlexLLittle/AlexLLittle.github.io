Doctor Who: 11th Doctor Adventures

Return to Skaro

“Well Sarah Jane, one more trip then I should be taking you home” the Doctor said pulling a lever in the TARDIS causing it to dematerialize, “I’ll bring you back November 17th 2010, the same day we left”

“That would be wonderful” said an older Sarah Jane Smith, no longer the same woman that was left in the wrong place by the 4th doctor, “just make sure its the correct location”

“How many times do I need to apologize for that? Jo never made a fuss like that when she was traveling with us”

“No she didn’t, but she was right to leave. We are getting too old for this”

“Nonsense, I’m thousands of years old and look at me!”

“Thousands of years old and yet now you have catchphrases…”

“It’s because bowties are cool. Nobody respects the bowtie”

“Oh Doctor, I am going to miss this. What are you going to do after you drop me off?”

“Amy and Rory still think I’m dead. Probably, so maybe I will drop in there, maybe do something else. I don’t know yet.” The TARDIS finally landed and the Doctor went to open the doors, outside was a desolate wasteland, “come on, lets see whats out here”

“Everything looks so dead Doctor”

“It doesn’t look that bad. Ok maybe it does but I’m sure there will be something alive sooner or later”

“I have the strangest feeling that I have been here before. I don’t like it, maybe we should try somewhere else” The Doctor shrugs off Sarah's request and keeps walking. The more he walks the more he agrees with Sarah.

“Ok, maybe we should go, although I am curious. Let's take a rest here and I’ll decide in a minute or so.”

Should the Doctor and Sarah retreat to the TARDIS?
