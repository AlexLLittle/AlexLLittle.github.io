Return to Skaro

“Is that all you want Davros?” the Doctor asked, “I can give you so much more”

“Doctor?” Sarah asked,

“What can you give me?”

“Well us two, we shouldn’t be here, we are from after the time war you see. You Daleks, you don’t win. I mean I, A TIMELORD, am standing on Skaro”

“You will tell me what went wrong!”

“No”

“You will or the girl dies!”

“Doctor!”

“You think I care when the fate of the universe is at stake?”

“Doctor, do not think of me as a fool. You could have avoided mentioning that at all with no threat to your companions life, you know something, and you will tell me”

“Nobody wins”

“You expect me to believe that?”

“Fine. I win. I am the only winner!” The Doctor snaps his fingers opening the TARDIS doors and kicks Davros in, “Sarah fast!” The two run into the TARDIS and close the doors,

“Doctor, what are you going to do with Davros in the TARDIS?”

“So this is what your capsule looks like Doctor”

“Shut up Davros!” The Doctor wipes his hand across the computer on Davros’ chair, causing DDavros to panic trying to flip everything back,

“What did you do to him, Doctor?”

“Flipped off all his life support, he wont die unless he doesn’t turn it back on but it gives us time to talk without worrying about him. I say we find another me and use their help to defeat Davros once and for all. It won’t change the outcome of the time war I don’t think, but it is worth a try! I will use the TARDIS Telepathic Interface to home in on myself!”

“Will that work Doctor?”

“Let's find out, GERONIMO!” the Doctor shoves his hands into the Telepathic Circuit and the TARDIS takes off, Davros is still flipping through all his switches,

“What will we do once we arrive?”

“We need to get out of the TARDIS, we can shove him out, or we can hope he will follow us. I will quickly flip some switches so that the TARDIS will only respond to me!”
