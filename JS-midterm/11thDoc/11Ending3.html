Return to Skaro

“Yeah you are right Sarah, it isn’t the best idea, let's get out of here”

“Excuse me?” the past incarnation said,

“You heard me. You are the one here, he is now here, it is your problem”

“Doctor!” Sarah said to him,

“Sarah, I can’t let you get hurt on your last trip lets go”

“No Doctor, I’m staying here, with the other Doctor, if you won’t fight Davros, I will”

“Well, goodbye then Sarah Jane Smith” feeling defeated the Doctor walked back to his TARDIS before seeing the other Doctor and their two companions huddled together thinking of a plan. As he enters the TARDIS he flips a few levers and decides to not see Amy and Rory again, at least not yet.

The End
