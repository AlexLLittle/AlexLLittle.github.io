Doctor Who: 11th Doctor Adventures

Return to Skaro

“Sarah, I don’t think it matters if we stay in the greenery or not. We should just make a run for the TARDIS. Hopefully they haven’t found it yet but I don’t have high hopes.” the two of them continued to run until eventually they could see a faint object in the distance,

“Doctor look!”

“That must be the TARDIS!” the two began to run towards it but as they approached they saw that is was surrounded by Daleks,

“Back away Sarah before they-”

“YOU WILL WALK TOWARDS THE TARDIS!”

“Nevermind Sarah. Yes you metal blocks, we will walk towards my TARDIS and you cannot stop us!”

“Wise choice Doctor” an old metallic voice said from behind the TARDIS,

“Davros”

“I see that you have regenerated Doctor, I approve of your new face, and is that Sarah Jane Smith?”

“Yes it is, Doctor what are we going to do?” Sarah asked the Doctor,

“What can we even do Sarah?”

“You will SURRENDER to me!” Davros declared!
